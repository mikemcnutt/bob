Module Overview

Welcome to CIT 160. This week we take the Syllabus Quiz, the Preassessment quiz and work through TestOut chapter 1.1-2.1. Chapter 1 will give us an overview of networking, the OSI Model, data encapsulation, and network protocols.

Why It Matters

Understanding the basics of networking is crucial as it forms the backbone of modern communication. The OSI Model, data encapsulation, and network protocols are fundamental concepts that enable different systems to communicate seamlessly. Mastering these topics provides a strong foundation for all future learning in computer networking, which is essential for anyone pursuing a career in IT or related fields.

Demonstrate Competencies

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

  • Explain the purpose and functions of the OSI Model.
  • Describe the process of data encapsulation in networking.
  • Identify key network protocols and their roles.
  • Apply these concepts to analyze simple network scenarios effectively.

These competencies will ensure you have the necessary knowledge to move forward in more complex networking topics.
