Printing


Each semester you will receive $15 on your printing account. Your balance will be available the second week of classes. If you do not use all of the funds in your print account that amount is carried over from fall to spring to summer. All print accounts will be reset to $15 at the beginning of the next school year. If you use all of your print account funds, you can add to your account at the Business Office or Library. New students will receive an email with their print code (also called a PIN).


Internet / WiFi Access


In order to connect to KCTCS wireless, select KCTCS-Guest from the selection menu on your wireless device. No password is required. You can also connect to KCTCS wireless from the MCTC parking lots closest to a campus building.

Are you having issues with Blackboard? For Blackboard assistance, you can access the Blackboard Help Guides or Live Chat Help. The chat feature is available from any page in Blackboard by clicking the help icon, which can be found at the bottom right of any page in Ultra.



Publisher Support


If you are having difficulty accessing a publisher's content, follow these steps:


    1. 1. First, let your instructor know you are having difficulty and that you are seeking support from the publisher. This not only lets your instructor know why they aren't seeing any work from you, but may also make them aware of an issue with their course.

    1. 2. Try testing your access in another browser. Usually Chrome and Firefox are the most reliable browsers. You may also wish to test your access via the Blackboard app on your mobile device.

  1. 3.Contact the publisher if the problem persists. You will receive the most accurate assistance when you contact the publisher directly for technical assistance. Many publishers offer a help desk chat feature which can provide you with instant support. Be prepared to supply important information about the course when you contact support (e.g., instructor name, course name, access code, error received, browser being used).
